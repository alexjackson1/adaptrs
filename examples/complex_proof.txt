# A more complex adaptive logic proof
# This example demonstrates both disjunctive syllogism and ex falso quodlibet

1. P ∨ Q                       PREM
2. Q → R                       PREM
3. ¬P                          PREM
4. Q                           1,3 DS {(P ∨ Q) ∧ ¬P ∧ ¬Q}
5. R                           2,4 MP
6. P ∧ ¬P                      PREM
7. S                           6 EFQ {P ∧ ¬P}
